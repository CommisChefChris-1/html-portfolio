About Me

With over 20 years in the kitchen, I’ve mastered more than just cooking. My journey from leading teams in fast-paced culinary environments to founding Savor the Passion has given me a deep understanding of efficient workflows, process optimization, and the art of training staff to excel quickly. I’ve spent over a decade focusing on building systems that streamline operations, allowing teams to work smarter, not harder, and ensuring consistent growth and quality.

As I transition from the culinary world into tech, I’m excited to bring my passion for problem-solving and efficiency to new challenges. I thrive on creating processes that make life easier for others, whether that’s in a kitchen or through innovative technology. My experience in founding Savor the Passion, where I brought chefs into homes across the country, has fueled my desire to continue sharing knowledge and providing value—this time, in the tech space.

I’m particularly drawn to robotics, but I’m open to any opportunity that allows me to tackle difficult problems and contribute to meaningful, lifestyle-enhancing solutions. I’m a chef at heart—one who believes that work should be both fun and effective, and that success comes from a combination of creativity, dedication, and a well-prepared plan.
